Buffalo Chicken Dip

A simple, five ingredient Buffalo chicken dip

Ingredients

  1. 1 can of fire roasted chicken
  2. 1 slab of creamcheese
  3. 1/4 cup of ranch dressing
  4. 3 TBSP of hotsauce
  5. 1 bag of shredded cheese

Instructions

Honestly, instructions for this dish can not be more simple! Start by mixing all the ingredients in a large mixing bowl. Then, spread the contents of the bowl into a medium sized baking dish. Set oven to 325 and place your baking dish inside for 15-20 minutes. Top with shredded cheese and eat with chips or crackers!
